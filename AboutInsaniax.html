Our Vision.

Our mission have always been about building a dancing community within our college. Our Club is a space for anyone and everyone who loves to dance. It doesn’t matter if you’re a pro dancer or have never danced before, all we look for is the determination and dedication to dance. With all of life’s burnout, Insaniax is where one can set aside their thoughts and can dance whole-heartedly. Dancing is ingrained in our hearts and minds and we are highly devoted towards it.

About Robotics Club.

Among the most active and intuitive clubs of the college, Insaniax, the dance club established in 2015, is a platform to climb the ladder and showcase yourself to the people with help of your dance. Be it Western or Indian form of dance, diversity is welcomed with open arms, cultivating a unique culture. This is an incredible platform not only for experienced dancers but also those who have the urge to learn, under this club we conduct daily sessions for them. It’s time to get your dance mood out of your room and step up the game, take over the stage!

Membership.

The selection process involves 2 rounds. In first round, interested contestants are supposed to prepare a dance on any song upon their discretion where as in second round, qualified contestants from round 1 have to perform on song(s) given by the judges to check their versatility and energy. The selection committee involves senior most members of the club.
