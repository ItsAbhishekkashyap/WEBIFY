Our Vision.

The club aims to encourage more and more students to develop their skills in various fields of robotics like electronics, mechanics, and programming. Students will learn to apply their theoretical knowledge with hands-on application. The club provides exposure to practical application and to ask students to look for solutions to various daily life problems. The purpose of the club is to enable students to utilize and enrich their creativity and learn qualities such as teamwork and leadership in them and help students to learn in a community and learn using hands-on projects and gain confidence by taking part in various competitions. The club wants to make engineers who can connect an idea to a working project, and help construct a better and constructive society .

About Robotics Club.

In IET LUCKNOW it all started in the year 2017. Here with Embedded systems, Robotics and Coding, by working individually, in groups on various projects, seeking help on the internet and from seniors, we finally started a community of enthusiastic Engineers as IET Robotics Club for hands on practical engineering experience in college.We at Robotics Club love to make Drones, Line following Robots and many more complex Projects and our achievements span from scoring top positions at top competitions all over India, while our club members score Various Internship and Job opportunities in prestigious companies.

Membership.

All incoming sophomores receive membership status when they first become a part of the robotics club and take part in robotics classes organized by the club. Members who complete at least one project during their first year, have a minimum of 80% attendance and clear the Interview taken by 2nd Year Mentors are eligible to become permanent members of Club.
