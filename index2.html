Excelsior Literary Club of IET Lucknow.

EXCELSIOR
Originally founded in 2014, the club houses students with a special
interest in writing, debating, policy drafting, quizzing, and public
speaking. Cutting across all four years and eight branches, the name
“Excelsior” itself stands for “superior quality”. In the same vein,
the members of the club consistently work to refine and polish their
skills to live up to this title. The club is home to students having a
passion for literature, debating, poetry writing, creative writing,
elocution etcetera; and to showcase talent. It is largely viewed as a
unique platform for all the literary enthusiasts in IET Lucknow,
whether one prefers the medium to be English or Hindi.

Parmarth Club of IET Lucknow.

PARMARTH
Parmarth is a student-driven initiative aspiring to educate the
underprivileged in the vicinity of campus. 147+ children living in
the Mohibullapur slum area have been admitted to private schools
through RTE with the efforts of this club. Student volunteers
(currently a team of 200+) organize elementary education classes
in the LT complex for 150+ such students.
Girls' education is emphasized upon and club members even
provide free home tuition to uneducated adolescent/adult
females, breaking the previously set stereotypes and creating a
brighter future for them.

Fine arts Club of IET Lucknow.

KALAKRITI
Fine Art is that confine in which the hand, the head and the heart of a person go together.
Kalakriti, the fine arts team of IET nourishes future picassos, helps them to give perfection to
their brush strokes, sharpness to their pencil and colours to their imaginations. One
of the oldest clubs of the college. Previously known as Fine Arts
Team of the college. Adopted the name Kalakriti in year 2017.
One of the most celebrated cultural club of the college.
The most famous “TIGER CHAURAHA” of the college was named after
the street painting of a tiger by Kalakriti.

Western Music Club of IET Lucknow.

AURORAS
Founded in 2015, the club introduced this subtle genre of music in the college which
had been missing, unlike other Institutes. As part of the Western Music Club,
the campus’ most musically talented residents come under one roof to cover everything
from Pink Floyd to Coldplay and Metallica to Linkin Park. It facilitates regular practice
sessions throughout the year for several competitions like Maruti Suzuki’s
Colors of youth, Indian Idol and cultural fests of various colleges.
The competitive academic environment and co-curricular activities
on campus keep the students on their toes.

Student Media Body Club of IET Lucknow.

WHITEBOARD
